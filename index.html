Pig Dice

Instructions

Roll The Dice...

  1. If a one is showing, end your turn and lose any points you accumulated this turn.
  2. If a one is not showing, add the total to your points for this turn.
  3. When you feel you've earned enough points to be safe, press 'hold' to pass the dice and secure your points.
  4. The first player to reach 100 points is the winner!
